{% extends 'layouts/slide.html' %} {% block contents %} # Theorem 9
There is a sequence of languages $ (L\_n)\_{n \ge 1} $ over a one letter alphabet such that $ nsc(L\_n) = \Omega \left( d\_n^{-\frac{1}{2} } 2^{d\_n} \right) $ where $d\_n$ is the bipartite dimension of $G_{L_n}$.
(where $G_{L_n}$ is a bipartitie graph called the "dependency graph" of L, defined in the paper) {% endblock %}
